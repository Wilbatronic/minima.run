# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Overview

Minima ("we", "our", "the app") is designed with privacy as a core principle. All AI processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Screen content or screenshots
- Keyboard input or typed text
- Files or documents you work with
- Conversations with the AI assistant
- Location data
- Contacts or calendar information

## Data We May Collect (Optional)

If you opt in to crash reporting:
- Anonymous crash logs
- Device model and OS version
- App performance metrics

This data is used solely to improve app stability and is never linked to your identity.

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple (Sign in with Apple)**: Used for authentication. Apple's privacy policy applies.
- **Apple App Store**: Used for subscription management. Apple's privacy policy applies.

## Screen Recording & Accessibility

Minima requests these permissions to:
- **Screen Recording**: Allow the AI to "see" your screen and provide contextual assistance.
- **Accessibility**: Enable hands-free automation features.

This data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted externally.

## Your Rights

You may:
- Revoke permissions at any time in System Settings
- Delete all local data by uninstalling the app
- Request information about any data we hold (we hold none)

Questions? Contact us at: privacy@minima.run
